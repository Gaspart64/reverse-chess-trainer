REVERSE-INCREMENTAL LEARNING:
  Instead of solving the full puzzle from the start, you:
  
  Level 1: Play only the FINAL move
           (You see the winning position and make the last move)
  
  Level 2: Play the final 2 moves
           (See the setup before the final move)
  
  Level 3: Play the final 3 moves
           (See deeper structure)
  
  ... continue adding moves until you solve from the start
  
  This builds chess intuition BACKWARDS = more effective learning!

USER FLOW:
  1. Load PGN file â†’ app extracts moves
  2. Click puzzle â†’ board shows starting position
  3. Play level 1 (final move only)
  4. If correct â†’ advance to level 2
  5. Level 2 = final 2 moves
  6. Progress through all levels
  7. Finish puzzle when you can play from the start
  8. Move to next puzzle
